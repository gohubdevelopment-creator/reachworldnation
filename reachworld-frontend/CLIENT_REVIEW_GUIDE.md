## üé® Design Elements to Review

### Colors
Your brand colors have been implemented:
- **Royal Blue (#0033A0)** - Primary color for headers, buttons
- **Gold (#FFD700)** - Accent color for highlights, CTAs
- **White (#FFFFFF)** - Background, clean spaces
- **Deep Charcoal (#0D0D0D)** - Text color
- **Sky Blue (#00BFFF)** - Secondary accents

**Questions:**
- Do these colors represent your brand well?
- Would you like any adjustments?

### Typography
- **Font**: Inter (modern, clean, professional)
- **Hierarchy**: Clear distinction between headings and body text

**Questions:**
- Is the text easy to read?
- Are font sizes appropriate?

### Animations
- **Smooth transitions** between sections
- **Scroll-triggered animations** (elements appear as you scroll)
- **Hover effects** on buttons and cards
- **Auto-playing carousel** in hero section

**Questions:**
- Are animations too fast/slow?
- Do they enhance or distract from content?
